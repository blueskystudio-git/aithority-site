Aithority is an AI-powered growth agency. We deliver SEO, paid media, custom web development, and marketing automation for multi-location businesses and ambitious brands ready to dominate search.

Aithority is the client-facing brand. Blue Sky Studio, LLC is the operating entity behind it and does not appear in client-facing materials.

### Why getaithority.co is canonical
The `.co` TLD carries the brand through both an agency phase and a future SaaS phase without a rename. "Get Aithority" functions as a CTA embedded in the URL, is shorter to say out loud, and avoids the redundancy of `.agency` after a brand name that already contains "authority." aithority.agency remains owned and 301-redirects to preserve any equity built against that domain.

## 4. Voice and Tone

Aithority speaks with earned confidence and a small amount of swagger. Short, declarative sentences. Serif italics as a rhetorical accent. We know what we're doing and we don't pad the language.

### The signature verbal move
On the site, every H1 and H2 pairs a bold sans-serif statement with one italicized serif phrase. The italic carries the promise. Examples:

- **Search.** *Built* **by machines. Run by experts.**
- **Every lever.** *Pulled together.*
- **Two tracks.** *One platform.*
- **Intake. Build.** *Ship. Optimize.*
- **Built on the** *right tools.*
- **Let's build your** *growth engine.*

**Rule:** every major headline uses this pattern. One italic phrase per headline, no more. The italic is the emphasis — don't dilute it.

### Voice traits
- **Clear over clever.** If a multi-location operator can't understand it in one read, rewrite it.
- **Evidence over adjectives.** "170 neighborhood pages, #1 in 14 markets" beats "industry-leading."
- **Confident, not arrogant.** Show, don't claim.
- **Modern, not futuristic.** We use AI; we don't fetishize it.
- **Short sentences.** Then shorter.

### Words we use
Authority. Signal. Compound. Engine. Intent. Citation. Lever. Velocity. Pipeline. Stack. System. Dominate. Scale. Platform. Flywheel.

### Words we avoid
Revolutionary. Cutting-edge. Disruptive. Synergy. Game-changing. "Unlock." "Harness." "Empower." Anything a chatbot would generate unprompted.

### Tone by surface
- **Homepage:** Declarative. Confident. Short. Italic accents carry the weight.
- **Case studies:** Numbers-forward. Specific. Structural.
- **Sales calls / intake:** Consultative. Question-led. Calm.
- **Dashboard UI:** Quiet. Functional. No marketing copy.
- **Client emails:** Direct. No fluff. Action-oriented.

## 5. Visual Identity

This is the locked v1 system as shipped on the live site.

### Color tokens

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| `--black` | `#050506` | Primary background |
| `--black-2` | `#0c0c0e` | Elevated surfaces, cards |
| `--white` | `#F2F0EB` | Primary type, warm off-white (not pure #FFF) |
| `--muted` | `#888885` | Secondary type, labels |
| `--muted-2` | `#555552` | Tertiary type, borders-on-type |
| `--accent` | `#C8FF00` | Electric lime — CTAs, italics, eyebrows, emphasis |
| `--accent-dim` | `#a8d600` | Accent hover state |
| `--accent-glow` | `rgba(200,255,0,0.08)` | Hover surfaces |
| `--border` | `rgba(242,240,235,0.1)` | Hairline rules |
| `--border-strong` | `rgba(242,240,235,0.18)` | Card hover borders |
| `--surface` | `rgba(242,240,235,0.03)` | Card hover fills |

**The lime accent (#C8FF00) is the brand.** It shows up on CTAs, eyebrow labels, italic serif phrases, tier prices, list bullets, and hover states. Use it sparingly — it earns its power from restraint.

### Typography

| Role | Font | Weights | Notes |
|------|------|---------|-------|
| Display / body | Inter | 300, 400, 500, 700, 900 | Near-universal, stacked against system fallbacks |
| Serif italic accent | Playfair Display | 400 italic only | Used exclusively in italicized H1/H2 phrases |

**Type rules:**
- Display headlines use 900 weight, tight tracking (-0.035 to -0.04em), line-height 0.9–0.95
- Eyebrows and section labels use uppercase, 0.22–0.28em tracking, 700 weight
- Body copy is 300 weight, 1.75 line-height, muted color
- Monospace is used for step numbers and service numbers (system mono stack)
- Never mix Playfair outside of italic phrases inside a headline — it's a single-purpose typeface

### Layout and motion

- **Grid background** in the hero: 80px × 80px hairline grid with a radial mask softening it at the edges
- **Radial lime blob** top-right of hero and bottom of CTA section for warmth
- **Fade-in on scroll** via IntersectionObserver, staggered by sibling index (80ms delay per item, max 400ms)
- **Hairline borders everywhere** — 0.5px rules separate sections; on hover, stronger
- **Service card signature:** top border animates in from left on hover (scaleX transform, 350ms cubic-bezier)
- **Generous vertical rhythm** — 8rem section padding, 14rem top on hero

### Logo / wordmark

- Current: "AITHORITY" uppercase, 900 weight, 0.18em letter-spacing
- Signature: the "O" is colored `--accent` (lime) — this is the mark
- Footer variant: "AITH**O**RITY" with the O in lime, same treatment
- **The colored O is the whole logo system right now.** If a standalone mark is developed later, it should reference the same letterform — an "O" ring, a pillar cross-section, or a flywheel cross-view.

### Iconography

- Geometric unicode glyphs used throughout for service and tech-stack items: ◎ ▲ ⬡ ⟳ ◈ ◐ ◉ ◇
- Low-key, monospace-adjacent, mechanical
- **Do not replace with illustrated or colorful icons.** The restraint is the style.

### Imagery
- Editorial photography over stock when used
- Clients, work, dashboard screenshots > abstract AI imagery
- The dark background + lime accent + hairline rules is strong enough to carry pages without photo
